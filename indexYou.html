Enkephalin

- Upon successful work with an Abnormality, PE Boxes (Positive Enkephalin) are produced. These are vital.

- You will be given a quota of Enkephalin to produce every day.

- We MUST reach this quota, or your workday will not end. For all employees, the day's work only ends when the quota is reached.

NOTE: We WILL keep you here until the daily quota is met. Your lawyers cannot stop us.

E.G.O. Equipment

NOTE: You are not permitted to take your E.G.O. home with you.
