Workplace Conduct

- There is no orientation day. You will have to meet your fellow employees on your own time.

- No running in the halls (unless a breach has occurred).

- Shouting is discouraged. Several Abnormalities react violently to noise.

- Abuse of the Tool Abnormalities will not be tolerated.

- Death is inevitable, so workplace relationships are discouraged.

NOTE: Friendship is strictly prohibited.

Work Orders

- Collectively, your job is to safely manage the Abnormalities when ordered to do so.

- There are 4 types of work orders; Instinct, Insight, Attachment and Repression.

- Every Abnormality has a preferred work type out of the four and have different ways to interact with it to minimize casualties.

NOTE: When you have no active Work Orders, unless ordered otherwise, you are expected to mop the floors.
